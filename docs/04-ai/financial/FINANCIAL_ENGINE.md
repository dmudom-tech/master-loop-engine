# Purpose

Financial Engine evaluates the financial quality, sustainability, profitability, valuation, growth, and resilience of every listed company.

Unlike Market Intelligence, which measures external market conditions, Financial Intelligence evaluates the intrinsic quality of the business.

Financial Engine is the foundation of long-term investment decisions and institutional stock selection.
