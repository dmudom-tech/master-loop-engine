# Purpose

Growth Engine evaluates the long-term growth potential, sustainability, and scalability of a company.

Unlike Revenue Engine, which focuses on historical revenue expansion, Growth Engine measures the company's future ability to increase revenue, earnings, cash flow, intrinsic value, and shareholder wealth.

The engine identifies businesses capable of delivering sustainable compound growth over multiple economic cycles.

Growth Engine is one of the primary drivers of long-term investment returns.
