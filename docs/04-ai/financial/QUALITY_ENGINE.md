# Purpose

Quality Engine evaluates the long-term quality, durability, and competitive strength of a business.

Unlike Profitability Engine, which measures current financial performance, Quality Engine determines whether the business possesses characteristics that enable sustainable long-term value creation.

The engine identifies institutional-grade businesses capable of compounding shareholder wealth over many years.

Quality is one of the most important drivers of long-term investment returns.
