# Purpose

Revenue Engine evaluates the quality, sustainability, consistency, and growth of a company's revenue.

Revenue is the first indicator of business expansion and market demand.

The engine analyzes not only how much revenue grows, but also whether the growth is sustainable, diversified, recurring, and profitable.

Revenue Engine is one of the primary inputs to

• Financial Score

• Growth Engine

• Quality Engine

• Earnings Engine

• AI Committee
